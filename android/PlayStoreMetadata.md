## Short description (≤ 80 chars)

## Full description (English, ≤ 4000 chars)
Gevabal helps you connect with Buddhist monks for guidance and consultations. Browse monk profiles, explore services, book sessions, and receive reminders so you don’t miss your scheduled time.

Key features:
- Browse monk profiles and services
- Book consultations and manage your schedule
- Receive reminders and important updates (notifications)
- Secure sign-in and account management
